BIAB (Builder In A Bottle) is a strengthening builder gel applied directly to natural nails. It protects and adds structure while looking natural — great for nail biters or those wanting stronger nails without extensions.

With good aftercare, gel nails typically last 2–3 weeks. BIAB overlays can last even longer. We recommend booking an infill every 3–4 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
