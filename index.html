Good to know

BIAB (Builder In A Bottle) is a strengthening builder gel applied directly to natural nails. It adds structure and protection while looking natural — great for nail biters or anyone wanting stronger nails without extensions.

With good aftercare, gel typically lasts 2–3 weeks; BIAB overlays often longer. We recommend an infill every 3–4 weeks to keep everything looking fresh.
